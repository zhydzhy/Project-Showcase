Title: The Windows Rant
Date: 2025-09-06

Every time I boot into Windows, I get nudged toward Bing, Edge, cloud features, and recommendations I never asked for. The Start menu feels less like a launcher and more like ad inventory.

Even search does not want to search *my* files first. It wants to search the web, push suggestions, and "help" in ways that slow down the one task I opened it for.

The more the platform tightens its grip on telemetry, ads, and account requirements, the more Linux feels like the exit.
