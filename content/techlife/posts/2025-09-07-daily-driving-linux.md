Title: Daily Driving Linux
Date: 2025-09-07

I use **[Linux](https://linux.org)** as my daily driver (Arch, btw). It is fast, minimal, and predictable in a way Windows has stopped being. I decide what runs, what updates, and what stays off the machine.
It was not a graceful transition. I have broken my desktop, damaged my bootloader, and wrecked drivers by "just updating one thing." That pain was still worth it because the system now feels like mine.
